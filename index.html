About Me

I am an engineering professional with a strong background in Mechanical, Metallurgy, and Materials Science. I hold a Master of Engineering in Mechanical from the University of Windsor and a Master of Technology in Metallurgical and Materials Science from the National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur.

My expertise spans stress analysis, non-linear analysis, and thermal analysis, with a keen interest in composites and CAD. I am proficient in metallurgical equipment like X-ray diffraction analysis, Scanning Electron Microscopy, and Differential Scanning Calorimetry, and I am fluent in English, Hindi, and Punjabi.
